Bud Sasser (born May 29, 1992) is a former American football wide receiver. He played college football at Missouri. He was drafted by the St. Louis Rams in the sixth round of the 2015 NFL Draft. On June 4, 2015, Sasser was waived by the Rams without ever officially signing his rookie contract, due to a pre-existing heart condition. On August 25, 2015, the Rams announced that they had hired Sasser as their external football affairs coordinator. Bud Sasser is currently attending Mizzou Law and has earned his MEd.
